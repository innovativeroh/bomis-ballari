Middle School Programme

Our Middle School Curriculum amalgamates the curriculum of all the boards seamlessly, creating a unique, rigorous, and student-centric educational foundation.

Overview

The Middle School Curriculum amalgamates the curriculum of all the boards seamlessly and it is the major reason behind its uniqueness. We begin teaching separate subjects, but integration and interdisciplinary teaching approach continues to take place wherever appropriate.

Methodology

Our approach is student-centric where the onus of learning shifts to the student. We use constructive methods—taking the child from known to unknown, concrete to abstract, and local to global. Innovation is key through active learning and inquiry-based activities.

Subject Offerings

We take students away from rote learning, encouraging them to go beyond textbooks and establish real-life connections.

Soul Science

A holistic and dynamic approach where children learn about ‘soul science’ to deal with different emotions, challenges, stress, anxiety and social insecurity. It teaches leadership, motivation, and how to regulate personal and school life through time management.

Assessments

We believe in assessing children on a regular basis using a variety of tools and techniques through Ongoing and Term End Assessments.

Ongoing Assessment

Occurs regularly throughout the instructional process. Periodic tests include a range of tools like rubrics, checklists, projects, tasks, and written tests.

Term End Assessment

A test given at the end of each term. These are paper-pencil tests that include questions requiring comprehension, analysis, and critical thinking.
