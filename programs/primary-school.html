Primary School Programme

The Primary Years curriculum is carefully designed to nurture the child in all dimensions of life to bring out the essence of a holistic being.

Our Educational Approach

At BOMIS, the curriculum is designed to fit the necessities, interests, capabilities, and learning styles of each child. We focus on boosting the social, emotional, spiritual, physical, motor, and cognitive development of our BOMISites, who are the centre of our educational system.

Assessment as a Journey

Assessment is an ongoing process that guides learners. We use Theme End and Term End Assessments to ensure timely feedback on achievement, shared through detailed report books and portfolios.

Ongoing Feedback

Regular updates shared through documented work portfolios, making learning visible to parents and teachers alike.

Timely Support

Detailed achievement feedback helps provide exactly the right challenge or support as per each child's individual need.
