Primary School (Grades 1-5)

The Primary Years curriculum is carefully designed to nurture the child in all dimensions of his/ her life to bring out the essence of a holistic being.

Scholastic

English, Hindi, Math, EVS, and ICT focus.

Co-Scholastic

Visual Arts, Performing Arts, Yoga, and Soul Science.

Program Hallmarks:

  • Thematic and integrated approach.
  • Valuing individuality and unique learning styles.
  • Ongoing and Term-End assessments for progress tracking.

Middle School Curriculum amalgamates the curriculum of all the boards seamlessly and it is the major reason behind its uniqueness.

Focus on emotional intelligence, leadership, and motivation.

Key Features:
